About the Conference

🔬

About VIVORYX

VIVORYX is a national-level academic conference organized to bring together students, researchers, academicians, and industry professionals in biotechnology. It encourages knowledge sharing, innovative thinking, and scientific discussions through Paper and Poster Presentations. Participants can showcase research ideas, learn about current developments, and interact with experts.

🏫

About the Department

The Department of Biotechnology at Jeppiaar Engineering College aims to produce bioengineers with academic excellence to meet global demands. Its mission is to provide quality education, transform technical knowledge into career opportunities, and bridge academia with society.

⚙️

About IEEE EMBS – JEC

The IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS) Student Branch Chapter at Jeppiaar Engineering College promotes the application of engineering in medicine and biological sciences. It organizes technical events, workshops, and conferences to enhance research and innovation and nurture future engineers.

Submission Guidelines

📄 Paper & Poster Guidelines

  • Participants: UG / PG / Research Scholars
  • Team size: Maximum 4 members
  • Paper structure: Title, Introduction, Objectives, Methodology, Results, Conclusion & References
  • PPT: 10–15 slides (visuals clear & readable)
  • Presentation time: 7 minutes + 3 minutes Q&A
  • Avoid reading from slides; clear explanation expected
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited

📝 Abstract Submission Guidelines

  • Word limit: 150–250 words
  • Must include: Background, Objective, Methods, Results/Findings & Conclusion
  • Abstracts screened by review committee
  • Selected abstracts notified via confirmation mail
  • Submit as .doc or .docx
  • Required fields: Title, Author Name(s), Designation & Affiliation, Email, Keywords

⚡ General Instructions

  • Topic must strictly follow Conference Theme / Sub-Themes
  • Decision of judges & organizing committee is final
  • Certificates provided to all participants
  • Best Paper & Best Poster Awards will be presented
